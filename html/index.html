What is Monthly Readers Club?

We make many excuses every day. "I'm tired" or "I'll do it tomorrow" can easily become a habit. When it comes to an important activity like reading, you shouldn't sacrifice the benefits just to make more time in your day. Because reading has been shown to reduce stress, improve mental abilities, and slow cognitive decline, Monthly Readers Club wants to make it as easy as possible to develop a reading habit.

Monthly Readers Club developed out of our love of books and reading. As life got busier, we found it more and more difficult to sit and read. We simply couldn't spare time to browse books on Amazon and who even goes to a book store nowadays. Monthly Readers Club aims to eliminate the time it takes to find great books by providing them for you so that you can invest your time in reading, not searching.
